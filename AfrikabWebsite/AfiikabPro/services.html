KEY

ARIKAB LIMITED is a company that prides itself in facilitating innovative approaches using its broad and experienced Consultants in the design and costing of infrastructure/building and its desire to ensure all projects are successfully completed within time-frame.

Our experience encompasses a wide range of projects and sizes. While various contractors/vendors can also be engaged on any given project, the specialized skills and neutrality which our experienced and certified project managers bring to the table will assist in achieving desired result.

It is important that an expert and competent organization like ours be engaged for the coordination of the different vendors to aid quality assurance and vetting of various designs including architecture and structural and other requirements submitted.

From statistics captured, at least two out of three projects ended up as failed due to not meeting one or more criteria. A successful project is defined as;

  • Achieved its strategic objectives
  • Produced/Completed the specified results on time
  • Delivered within the approved budget/cost.

ARIKAB LIMITED is a fast growing African based business and management practice; project, consulting, infrastructure deployment, serving clients throughout the continent.

We take pride in delivering world class services to our clients and, in so doing, contributing to the economic and social development of Africa. We are therefore pleased to submit our proposal to be engaged as Consultants, Architects and Project Managers for upcoming projects.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT ROLES

As project managers, our general brief should involve the following;

  • Co-ordination of Contractors/Vendor’s activities along with the implementation plan
  • Completion of project within schedule and budget
  • Monitoring and Reporting of project status
  • Coming up with standards for the infrastructure to be deployed
  • Quality assurance to ensure the project meets all the strategic objectives
  • Completion report stating the lessons learnt, learning points, etc.

The attendant deliverables on completion of this exercise includes various reports generated at each stage of the engagement and a comprehensive final report after the post implementation review.

The report objectives are to provide the Ministry of Works with clear and concise answers to the following questions:

  • What is the roadmap to be used in executing the infrastructure deployments?
  • How can selected best fit vendors be managed for effectiveness and timely delivery of project milestones?
  • Is there any scoring template to track vendor’s performance

QUALITY ASSURANCE / MANAGEMENT

ARIKAB LIMITED ensures a high-level of quality for the project deliverables and for project management. Activities include defining processes, procedures, standards and policies as required to satisfy project stakeholder needs.

Quality assurance is the set of systematically planned activities to provide confidence the deliverables and services of the project will fulfil the defined requirements for quality.

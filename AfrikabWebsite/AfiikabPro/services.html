PROJECT MANAGEMENT ROLES

As project managers, our general brief should involve the following;

  • Co-ordination of Contractors/Vendor’s activities along with the implementation plan
  • Completion of project within schedule and budget
  • Monitoring and Reporting of project status
  • Coming up with standards for the infrastructure to be deployed
  • Quality assurance to ensure the project meets all the strategic objectives
  • Completion report stating the lessons learnt, learning points, etc.

The attendant deliverables on completion of this exercise includes various reports generated at each stage of the engagement and a comprehensive final report after the post implementation review.

The report objectives are to provide the Ministry of Works with clear and concise answers to the following questions:

  • What is the roadmap to be used in executing the infrastructure deployments?
  • How can selected best fit vendors be managed for effectiveness and timely delivery of project milestones?
  • Is there any scoring template to track vendor’s performance

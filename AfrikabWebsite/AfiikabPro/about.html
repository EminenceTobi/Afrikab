Afrikab

World Class Services & Consultancy

BEST

Arikab Limited is a conglomerate with interest in diverse areas such as General sales/supplies, Information Technology, Construction/Engineering, Oil and Gas, Agriculture, Training, Consulting and Project Management.

We are fully indigenous organisation, incorporated under Nigeria law in February 2013, to provide the aforementioned services with provision for growth and expansion into other areas.

A limited liability company with paid up capital in excess of 10 million units, we have the experience and capacity to handle large scale transactions due to our track record.

The Group is constituted by experienced and knowledgeable professionals cutting across a wide range of industries such as manufacturing, financial, academia, real estate and general administration with bias for purchase/supply of goods and quality assurance.

We have successfully delivered projects and purchase orders (LPO) in all our areas of focus.

We boast of over 10 years combined practical experience (partners) along with certifications in our areas of focus.

We not only boast of implementations of projects in Nigeria, we have also carried out feasibility studies coming out with strategic plans leading to establishment of Nigerian Banks’ subsidiaries across the West Africa coast.

We have also played key roles in projects undertaken at both state and federal level.

Our Structure

BEST

ARIKAB LIMITED has a tri-service structure.

  • At the leadership level is the company’s board of directors headed by the Chairman/CEO
  • At the Executive Management, we have the Partners managing the following portfolios: Construction, ICT Operations, General Supplies/Sales and Consultancy/Project Administration.

We are also fully integrated into the project management delivery systems framework using Project Management Institute (PMI).

We have access to wide range of products/goods/services due to Memorandum of Understanding [MOU] with our working partners/distributors.

This has allowed us to leverage our brand and enable us deliver on projects and LPO within deadline.

A significant aspect of this MOU relates to technical support and facilitation services which our offshore partners will render to solution project in Nigeria under agreed terms of engagement.
